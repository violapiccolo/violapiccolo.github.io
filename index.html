Nika's Musical Journey

Hi, I'm Nika!

I started my piano journey when I was just 5 years old, and it has been the most amazing adventure! For over 12 years, I've been discovering the magic of music, and now I want to share that same joy and excitement with your little ones.

I absolutely love working with young children because I remember exactly what it felt like to sit at the piano for the first time. My goal is to make every lesson fun, encouraging, and filled with discovery so your child can fall in love with music just like I did!

Fun Learning Adventures

Little Sprouts

Ages 5-7

Perfect first piano experience! We use games, stories, and simple songs to introduce your little one to the magical world of music.

$30/30min

Little Sprouts Details

What We'll Learn:

  • Basic finger positions and posture
  • Simple melodies with fun stories
  • Musical games and activities
  • Beginning note recognition
  • Rhythm with clapping and movement

Perfect For:

  • Complete beginners
  • Short attention spans
  • Learning through play

Young Explorers

Ages 8-10

Ready to explore more? We'll learn popular songs, fun technique games, and start reading music together.

$45/45min

Young Explorers Details

What We'll Learn:

  • Popular songs they love
  • Basic music reading skills
  • Proper technique through games
  • Simple chord progressions
  • Performance confidence building

Perfect For:

  • Kids ready for more challenge
  • Building reading skills
  • Learning favorite songs

Rising Stars

Ages 11-14

For tweens and teens ready to shine! We'll work on their favorite songs, build confidence, and maybe even prepare for performances.

$60/hour

Rising Stars Details

What We'll Learn:

  • Advanced popular songs
  • Music theory concepts
  • Performance preparation
  • Personal style development
  • Recording and sharing music

Perfect For:

  • Tweens and teens
  • School performance prep
  • Building serious skills
